Myagile is a complete agile project management system (Scrum software) that synchronizes your project's requirements, tasks, issues, etc in one place.

Before using the system, you need to perform these basic steps follow:

  1. Login or register (if your first time).
  2. Create your project.
  3. Create and invite your members into your team.
  4. Create your product backlog which includes the user stories.
  5. Your team need to create the sprint to track the task and issue.
