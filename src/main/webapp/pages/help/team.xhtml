1. Create team

In “My Team” tab, click on “New Team” button

Fill in the form information and upload your team logo by clicking on "Change Logo" button

Team Name (*): name of your team. e.g: Ant, 7Up,..
Group Mail (*): group email of your team. The mail must be right format.
Description: write something about your team
Project: choose your exist project(s) performed

Note: (*) means required field.

Click on “Save” button. The created team is also added to table of teams

BACK TO TOP
2. Edit team

Select a team from “Select a Team” combo box, click on “Edit Team” button

Also, select a team from table of teams, click on “Edit” button (pencil symbol)

Likely creating a team, you can fill in the form information and upload your team logo for changes. Click on “Save” button.

Note: Click on project name to go to product backlog page

BACK TO TOP

3. Assign member to team

You can set up a team by inviting members via email. Select a team from “Select a Team” combo box. Enter an email or groups of email that its each mail is divided by a semicolon “;”. The email format must be full-filled.

Click on “Send Mail” button. The system will send the mail(s) to member(s).

The assigned members need to check their email and accept to join the team.

The member can login with the user name and password are provided and click on the link “click here” to active.

The member joined into the team.

BACK TO TOP
4. View team information

You can view the team information includes the members of team.
Beside that, you can assign or re-assign the role of a member

To remove a member from the team, you can move the mouse to the avatar of member and click on “Remove” link
