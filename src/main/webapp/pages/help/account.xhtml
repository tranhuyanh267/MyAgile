1.Login / Sign up
  • Sign up
    • Create a registration by your email and fill in the rest information.
    • Click the “Sign up” button. If account have been created successfully, the page will redirect to profile page
  • Profile
    • You have to check your email to confirm before using the system.
    • In the confirm email, click the “Click here” link. It will redirect to your profile page
    • Fill in your information then click update to update your profile.
  • Login
    • Input your email and password which you registered to start myagile
    • Click the "Login" button to login
    • Note: in Login form, if you input an email that is not registered yet, Sign Up form will be displayed to let you register.
2. Forget password

After entering your user name, you can click the “Forget Password” link..

Fill in the user name again and click the “Send mail” button.

You need to check your email to receive your new password. Don't forget click the active link.

3. Switch UserName (Email account)
  • Edit profile
    • Click on Edit profile and then you will see this page:
    • Click on the “Plus” icon and then you can fill your new email. Click on “Save” icon to save.
    • Message: add new email successfully. Then you have to check the email and activate.
  • Activate alternate email
    • In the confirm email, click the “Click here” link. It will redirect to successful page.
    • Active Successful Message:
  • Switch Primary Account
    • After active successful, you will see the alternate email and the switch icon.
    • Click on the “Switch” icon to set primary email for it

    • See the confirm dialog, click “Yes” to set your alternate email is the primary email.

    • It will redirect to login page and now you can login by your new email.
  • Successful
    • Successful! The new email is the primary email. Team can see your new email. This email will receive the emails from system.
