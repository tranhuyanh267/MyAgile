Choose “Statistics & Feedbacks” in menu to go to Statistics & Feedbacks page

1. View statistics information in sprint

Choose the sprint you want to view statistics

And generate (if the sprint have no statistics)

or re-generate the statistics (if you want come back to default statistics)

This is a statistics of sprint

You have a lot of fields (automatically figure out by system) and you can also change red rectangle field (Team size, sprint size ans available man day), green rectangle field (unit of field) and blue field (note for statistics)

  • Team size : all of members in team
  • Sprint size : total day of sprint (from start date to end date except Saturday and Sunday)
  • Available man day : team size * sprint size
  • Point : total point of all issues belong to sprint
  • User story : all issues belong to sprint
  • Velocity of sprint : total point / available man day
  • Note : note for sprint

* We have two types : Estimated (planed by team) and Delivered (accepted by PO)

You can update field by click on field, and the textbox is shown later. Press “Enter” to accept this update content.
