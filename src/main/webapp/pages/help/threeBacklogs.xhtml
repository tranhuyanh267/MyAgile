  1. Product backlog

    1. Create a new user story

      Step 1: Click "New" button

      Step 2: Fill in the form

      Step 3: Click "Save" button to finish.

    1. Update user story

      Step 1: Choose user story to update information and click the pencil button to show the update form

      Step 2: Fill in the update form

      Step 3: Click “Save” button to finish.

    1. Remove user story

      Step 1: Choose user story to update information and click the waste basket button

      Step 2: Confirm message before delete

    1. View information

      In left column on homepage you can see all information about user story

      By pagination, you can show 5, 10, 15, 30, 15 or all of user stories

      Click button to view detail

      or click the link

      Click this button to collapse or expand all user stories

    1. Filter user story

      You can filter user story by status (To do, In-Progress, Done, Void) or sort by create date, value, name (descend or ascend)

    1. Search user story

      Enter ID or subject of user story

  2. Sprint backlog

    1. In center column on homepage, choose sprint and date to see all information about sprint backlog.

      You can watch the video, upload a new video for selected date

      And the burndown chart

  3. Impediment backlog

    1. In right column on homepage you can see all impediment of team

      Unsolved impediment
      Solved impediment

      With creating, updating and deleting you can refer to Product backlog usage to do it.

      And you can solve the impediment by click this icon

  4. History

    1. Another function is History on home page. You can see the History which consists of three tabs. There are Project, Team and Issue.

      In Project tab of History, you can see all history of a team, which is chosen in the drop down list above. They show in 5 records, and whenever clicking Show more button, it will show more 5.

      In Team tab, there are all history about team and sprint, and all of its is chosen above too. About team, it shows all changes about team's and members' information. With sprint, all information about changes of sprint is displayed. Besides, there is a Show more button, and it works same, too.

      Finally, all history of issues in sprint is shown in Issue tab. Moreover, there are two date selectors for being easy to choose date start and date end for showing. Beside that, the Show more button is available and it works in the same way.

  5. Feedback

    1. Click on "Feedback" button to send your feedback.

      Input your idea.

      Click "Post idea" button to post your idea.
